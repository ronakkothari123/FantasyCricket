How do you play Fantasy Cricket?

New to Fantasy Cricket? Learn how to play and win!

Fantasy Basics

Fantasy Cricket is where you create your own team of players to play against your friends' teams in your league. You select your favorite players in the cricket draft orgainzed by the league manager. These players combine for a point total, and if your point total is higher than another players point total, you win the game. Players that win the most games go to the fantasy playoffs and then go onto win the Championship!

The Dashboard

The Dashboard will be where you can adjust your cricket lineup and do your league draft when it's decided on by your league manager. The Dashboard is an important part of your fantasy cricket experience, and will allow you to win games.

League Games/Roster

You will play games against other players in your league. Each of the players on your team and your opponents team will have to play a real-live cricket game once. When they all play, the points are calculated and then the winner of your fantasy game is declared.

The Points System

Each player you draft can be put a specific position, for example bowler, batsman, fielder, and keeper. A batsman's points are calculated by dividing the runs they score by 2. A bowlers score is calculated based on their economy, wickets, and extras. A fielder and keepers score is calculated based on their runouts, caught outs, and dropped catches

How To Win

You win by scoring more points and winning games. After winning games, the teams with the most points go into a bracket and play each other in a playoff-style, and the winner of this bracket becomes the league winner!
